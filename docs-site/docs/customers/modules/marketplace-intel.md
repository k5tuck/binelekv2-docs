# Marketplace Intelligence

Marketplace Intelligence helps you track competitor pricing, monitor market trends, and discover opportunities to optimize your business.

## Features

### Product Price Tracking
Monitor your products and compare prices against competitors.

**Supported Marketplaces:**
- Amazon
- Shopify stores
- Etsy
- eBay (coming soon)
- Walmart (coming soon)

### Competitor Monitoring
Track competitor stores and get alerts when they make changes.

**What We Track:**
- Price changes
- New product listings
- Promotional activity
- Stock availability

### Market Trends
Understand broader market trends in your category.

**Insights Include:**
- Average market price
- Price trends over time
- Seasonal patterns
- Demand indicators

### Price Alerts
Get notified when prices change significantly.

**Alert Types:**
- Competitor price drop
- Competitor price increase
- Stock availability changes
- New competitor products

## Getting Started

### Adding Products to Track

1. Go to **Marketplace Intel** in the sidebar
2. Click **Track Product**
3. Enter the product URL or search by name
4. Select which competitors to monitor
5. Set your alert preferences
6. Click **Start Tracking**

### Adding Competitors

1. Go to **Marketplace Intel**
2. Click **Add Competitor**
3. Enter the competitor's store URL
4. Select which products to track
5. Click **Save Competitor**

### Setting Up Alerts

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Find **Marketplace Intelligence** section
3. Configure your alert preferences:
   - Price change threshold (e.g., > 5%)
   - Alert frequency (immediate, daily digest)
   - Notification method (email, push)

## Understanding the Dashboard

### Price Comparison Table

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Product | Your product name |
| Your Price | Your current price |
| Competitor | Competitor's price |
| Difference | % difference (+ = you're higher) |
| Trend | Price movement direction |

### Key Metrics

- **Products Tracked** - Total products being monitored
- **Competitors** - Number of competitor stores tracked
- **Price Opportunities** - Products where you could adjust pricing
- **Alerts** - Unread price alerts

## Pricing Strategies

Use Marketplace Intelligence to implement these strategies:

### Competitive Pricing
Match or beat competitor prices on key products.

**Best for:** Commodity products, price-sensitive markets

### Value-Based Pricing
Position premium products above market average.

**Best for:** Unique products, strong brand recognition

### Dynamic Pricing
Adjust prices based on market conditions.

**Best for:** High-volume sellers, seasonal products

## Best Practices

1. **Focus on key products** - Track your top-selling products first
2. **Know your margins** - Don't just match prices; understand your costs
3. **Monitor trends** - Look at long-term trends, not just daily changes
4. **Act on insights** - Use the data to make informed pricing decisions
5. **Review regularly** - Check your dashboard at least weekly

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often is competitor pricing updated?**
A: Prices are checked every 4 hours for Pro plans, every 24 hours for Free plans.

**Q: Can I track products on any website?**
A: We currently support Amazon, Shopify, and Etsy. More marketplaces coming soon.

**Q: What if a competitor doesn't have a public price?**
A: We can only track publicly available prices. B2B or login-required prices are not supported.

**Q: Can I export pricing data?**
A: Yes! Click Export on any report to download as CSV or Excel.
