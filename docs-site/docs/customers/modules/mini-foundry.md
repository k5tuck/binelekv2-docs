# Mini Foundry

Mini Foundry is your business intelligence hub. It consolidates data from all your connected sources and presents it in beautiful, actionable dashboards.

## Features

### Data Consolidation
Connect multiple data sources and see all your business data in one place.

**Supported Sources:**
- E-commerce (Shopify, Square)
- Finance (QuickBooks, Xero, Stripe)
- CRM (HubSpot, Zoho)
- Email (Gmail, Outlook)

### Custom Dashboards
Build dashboards tailored to your business needs.

**Dashboard Types:**
- Sales Overview
- Customer Analytics
- Inventory Status
- Marketing Performance
- Financial Health

### Automated Reports
Schedule reports to be generated and delivered automatically.

**Report Options:**
- Daily sales summary
- Weekly performance digest
- Monthly executive report

## Getting Started with Mini Foundry

### Connecting Data Sources

1. Go to **Connectors** in Settings
2. Click on the service you want to connect
3. Follow the authentication steps
4. Wait for initial data sync (usually 5-15 minutes)

### Creating a Dashboard

1. Go to **Mini Foundry** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Dashboard**
3. Choose a template or start blank
4. Add widgets by clicking **+ Add Widget**
5. Configure each widget with your data
6. Save and name your dashboard

### Widget Types

| Widget | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| Line Chart | Trends over time |
| Bar Chart | Comparing categories |
| Pie Chart | Distribution/composition |
| Number Card | Single key metrics |
| Table | Detailed data lists |
| Map | Geographic data |

## Data Refresh

Data is synced automatically based on your plan:

| Plan | Sync Frequency |
|------|----------------|
| Free | Every 24 hours |
| Pro | Every 1 hour |
| Enterprise | Real-time |

You can also manually trigger a sync from the Connectors page.

## Best Practices

1. **Focus on what matters** - Don't track everything; focus on metrics that drive decisions
2. **Set up alerts** - Get notified when key metrics change significantly
3. **Share dashboards** - Invite team members to view dashboards relevant to their role
4. **Review regularly** - Schedule time to review your dashboards weekly

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How far back does historical data go?**
A: We import up to 2 years of historical data when you first connect a source.

**Q: Can I export data from dashboards?**
A: Yes! Click the download icon on any dashboard to export as CSV or PDF.

**Q: How do I share a dashboard with my team?**
A: Click Share on any dashboard and enter your team member's email address.
