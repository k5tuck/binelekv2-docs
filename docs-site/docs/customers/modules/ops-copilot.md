# Ops Copilot

Ops Copilot is your AI-powered operations assistant. It helps automate repetitive tasks, manage workflows, and streamline your daily business operations.

## Features

### Task Automation
Create automated tasks that run on a schedule or when triggered by events.

**Examples:**
- Send invoice reminders every Monday
- Follow up with leads 3 days after initial contact
- Generate weekly sales reports

### Email Automation
Let AI draft and send emails based on templates and business rules.

**Examples:**
- Welcome emails to new customers
- Order confirmation messages
- Payment reminder sequences

### Workflow Builder
Design multi-step workflows that connect your business processes.

**Examples:**
- New customer onboarding workflow
- Order fulfillment process
- Employee offboarding checklist

## Getting Started with Ops Copilot

### Creating Your First Automation

1. Go to **Ops Copilot** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Automation**
3. Choose a template or start from scratch
4. Configure your trigger (schedule, event, or manual)
5. Define the actions to perform
6. Save and activate

### Quick Actions

Ops Copilot provides quick actions for common tasks:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Send Email | Compose and send emails to customers or team |
| Schedule Meeting | Book calendar events with attendees |
| Create Document | Generate reports, invoices, or documents |
| Run Workflow | Execute a saved workflow manually |

## AI-Powered Suggestions

Ops Copilot learns from your business patterns and suggests automations that could save you time. Look for the **Suggestions** section on your dashboard.

## Best Practices

1. **Start simple** - Begin with one or two automations before building complex workflows
2. **Test thoroughly** - Use test mode before activating automations
3. **Monitor results** - Check the activity log to ensure automations work as expected
4. **Iterate** - Refine your automations based on results

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many automations can I create?**
A: Depends on your plan. Free plans include 5 active automations, Pro plans include unlimited.

**Q: Can I pause an automation temporarily?**
A: Yes! Click the pause button next to any automation to temporarily disable it.

**Q: What happens if an automation fails?**
A: You'll receive a notification, and the failed run will be logged for review.
