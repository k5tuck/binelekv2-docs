# Getting Started

Welcome to the SMB AI Command Platform! This guide will help you get up and running with our AI-powered business tools.

## What is SMB AI Command Platform?

SMB AI Command Platform is an all-in-one AI assistant for your business. It helps you:

- **Automate tasks** - Let AI handle repetitive work like sending emails and reminders
- **Visualize data** - See your business metrics in beautiful dashboards
- **Stay secure** - Monitor your cybersecurity posture and compliance
- **Track competitors** - Keep an eye on market prices and competitor activity

## Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Account

Visit [app.smb-ai-platform.com](https://app.smb-ai-platform.com) and sign up with your email address.

### 2. Set Up Your Organization

After signing up, you'll be prompted to create your organization:

1. Enter your company name
2. Select your industry
3. Choose your company size

### 3. Connect Your First Integration

The platform works best when connected to your existing business tools. Start by connecting one of these popular integrations:

- **Shopify** - Sync your e-commerce data
- **QuickBooks** - Import financial information
- **Gmail** - Enable email automation

Go to **Settings > Connectors** to connect your first integration.

### 4. Ask Your First Question

Once connected, try asking the AI assistant a question:

> "What was my revenue this month?"

The AI will analyze your connected data and provide an answer.
